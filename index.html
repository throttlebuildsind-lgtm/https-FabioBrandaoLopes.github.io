Materials / Experimental Testing

Impact Strength of Commercial PLA by Percent Infill

Supervised a team of 4 students in the design of 7 specimens and Izod impact testing of 3D-printed PLA parts using ASTM D256-style procedures to evaluate infill effects on impact resistance.

ASTM D256 Izod Testing 3D Printing PLA Data Analysis
Result: With a gyroid infill pattern, 60% infill produced the best impact strength while 50% infill was the weakest.
